üìã Important Rules & Guidelines

The 5 W's and H Rule

WHO: Who Placed the Order/Req (Name/Initials)

WHAT: What format did you make the request (Email, phone, fax, maxwell, bonzo)

WHEN: When did you make the request (Date/Time)

WHERE: Where did you send the request (Name of POC, Title, Number, Email, Fax#, Etc)

WHY: Why are you ordering it? (first time, corrections, clarifications, additional items)
